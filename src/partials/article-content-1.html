Help us recognize outstanding women in the workplace | Women Who Rock event

In celebration of Women’s History Month, we are honoring Volkswagen Women Who Rock! We are accepting nominations now through March 13. By submitting a name for consideration, you help us identify female role models with leadership potential!

Nominate today by…

  1. Scanning the QR code
  2. Visiting https://linktr.ee/WomenWhoRock
  3. Submitting a paper nomination form to the drop boxes in Paint and 10-meter level Main Stair well (available for hourly employees only. Forms are prefilled with employee information)

Employees who submit a nomination are entered to win an Apple iPad! Nomination winners receive over $200 in prizes and will be announced at the awards brunch on Saturday, March 21, 11 a.m. in the Volkswagen Conference Center. Questions? Email vwgoa.womeninmotion@vw.com.
